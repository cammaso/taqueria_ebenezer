OUR STORY

Ebenezer means Stone of Help — a reminder that God's faithfulness carries us through everything. Taqueria Ebenezer was born from that same spirit: a place where family, faith, and the best Mexican street food come together right here in Stephenville, Texas.

"Whatever you eat or drink, do it all for the glory of God." — 1 Corinthians 10:31

We're proud to call Stephenville home and to serve the Tarleton State community — students, families, and neighbors are always welcome at our truck. Come hungry, leave full, and know you're cared for.
